## Project Overview
UM Natural Hair Products is a web-based application designed to showcase and managea natural hair care products. The project aims to provide users with information about the products provided in store, their benefits, and  its usage instructions. It also includes features for product management, user interaction, and educational resources to promote healthy hair care practices.

## Website Goals & Objectives
The main goal of the website is to sell our products and to ensure that our customers get a better reach of the product from anywhere. This is to also help the customers who live far from where the retail store might be located and cannot purchase the product in store.
The present KPIs to measure success in our business would be sales, the businesses overall performance, monitoring the progress of our business as well as the finances of the business.

## Key Features & Functionality
Easy to use on phones/tablets.
Optimized images and smooth navigation.
Optimization- To reach more customers searching for kids’ natural hair care.
Instagram shop, Facebook store link, TikTok hair care tutorials.
Order Tracking – Customers can check delivery status online.
